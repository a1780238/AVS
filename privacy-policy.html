Your Privacy Rights (Australian Privacy Principles)

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), you have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of information that is inaccurate, out-of-date, incomplete, irrelevant, or misleading.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Privacy Officer (details below). We may need to verify your identity.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected (e.g., to complete your project, provide ongoing services) and to comply with our legal and record-keeping obligations under Australian law.
